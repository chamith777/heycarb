Our Products

HeyCarb offers a wide range of activated carbon solutions designed to meet diverse needs.

  • 🔹 Granular Activated Carbon (GAC)

    Ideal for water and wastewater treatment, offering high adsorption capacity and long service life.

  • 🔹 Powdered Activated Carbon (PAC)

    Perfect for rapid mixing systems in water treatment, beverage purification, and chemical industries.

  • 🔹 Pelletized Activated Carbon

    Designed for air and gas purification applications, including odor control and solvent recovery.

  • 🔹 Impregnated Activated Carbon

    Specially treated for enhanced removal of specific gases and contaminants in industrial use.
